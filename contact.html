Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make a donation?

You can make a donation through our secure online platform by visiting our Donate page. We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI payments through Razorpay.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a receipt via email for your records.

How can I volunteer?

You can register as a volunteer through our contact form or visit our office directly. We offer various volunteering opportunities based on your skills and availability.

How is my donation used?

We ensure maximum transparency in fund utilization. 85% of donations go directly to program expenses, 10% to administrative costs, and 5% to fundraising. We publish annual reports with detailed financial statements.
